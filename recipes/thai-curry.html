Red Thai Curry

Description

Thai curry. I've been eating the stuff since i was 6. I've got barely any surviving gut bacteria.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Red Curry Paste
  • Sesame Oil
  • All the veggies you like, oh, also coconut oil, i forgot about that.

Steps

  1. Sear chicken breast in sesame oil, then take out
  2. add all the sliced veggies and coconut milk and curry paste
  3. Simmer for a while, then spice to taste
